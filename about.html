Why Choose Us

Tailored Lessons: We understand that each student is unique, and that's why our guitar, bass, and ukulele lessons are fully tailored to meet each learner's specific needs and goals. We'll take the time to get to know you, your musical preferences, and learning style to ensure you get the most out of every lesson.

Professionally Trained Instructors: Seasoned musician and Aurora Music owner Ian Rafalak has over 18 years of experience teaching music privately and at the college level. Equipped with a Bachelor's in Music Education and Performance from Ithaca College and a Master's in Jazz Studies from the University of the Arts, Ian has had the good fortune to play with bands and artists such as Trinidelphia, Jeanette Berry and Soul Nerds, and Luke Slott, performing from New York to LA. He's even shared the stage with world class performers such as Melody Gardot and Anwar Marshall. With years of experience in instruction, performance and production, Ian is primed to help you achieve your full potential.

All Ages and Abilities: No matter your age or skill level, you can learn how to play the guitar, bass, or ukulele. Whether you're a young child, a teenager, an adult, or a senior, we have the expertise to provide age-appropriate instruction that suits your level of proficiency.

Variety of Genres: Whether you dream of playing rock, pop, jazz, blues, folk, or any other genre, our instructors can teach you the techniques and theory necessary to excel in your preferred style.
