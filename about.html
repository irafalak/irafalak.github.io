Our Staff

Ian

Ian Rafalak is a bassist, producer and educator based in Aurora, CO. A recent transplant from Philadelphia, his unique sound on the bass stems from his studies at the University of the Arts with world-renowned bassist Gerald Veasley, classical guitar studies with Pablo Cohen, and his time in Ghana studying traditional West African rhythms. Ian's work as a producer and orchestrator has included music for VH1 and the BBC. He's performed with artists/group such as Trinidelphia, Jeanette Berry and the Soul Nerds and Leana Song. He's taught privately for 18 years, including many years as a professor at Camden County College and is now building a thriving teaching studio in Colorado.
